ScrapeFlow lets you extract structured data from websites without writing code.

## FAQ (For Support)

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: ScrapeFlow only extracts publicly visible data. Always respect website terms of service and robots.txt.

**Q: Does it work on all websites?**
A: Works on most static websites. Some JavaScript-heavy sites may require Pro version.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All processing happens in your browser. We never see your data.

**Q: What's the difference from Octoparse?**
A: ScrapeFlow is 75% cheaper, runs as a Chrome extension, and has a simpler UI.

**Q: Can I schedule scrapes?**
A: Yes, with Pro version ($19/mo).
